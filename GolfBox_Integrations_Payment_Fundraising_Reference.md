## GOLFBOX - Complete Club & Tournament Management System
**Source:** https://golfbox.zendesk.com/hc/no

### What it is
GolfBox is the Scandinavian standard for golf club management software used by over 1,000+ golf clubs across multiple countries. It's a comprehensive, cloud-based platform specifically designed for Norwegian golf clubs and federations, serving as the central system for member management, bookings, tournaments, handicap administration, and financial operations.

**Target Users:**
- Golf clubs of all sizes (small to large)
- Golf federations
- PGA professionals
- Tournament organizers
- Individual golfers

### Key Features & Modules

#### Core Club Management
- **Member Management**: Complete member database, handicap tracking (WHS - World Handicap System accredited)
- **Booking System**: Tee time reservation management with flexible booking rules
- **Tournament Management**: Competition organization with scoring, handicap calculations, and results
- **Financial Management (Økonomimodulen)**: Invoice management, member billing, financial reporting
- **Communication**: Email and SMS notifications to members about bookings, tournaments, and events

## GRASROTANDELEN - Grassroots Funding for Clubs
**Source:** https://www.norsk-tipping.no/grasrotandelen/for-lag-og-foreninger

### What it is
Grasrotandelen (Grassroots Share) is a government-supported funding program administered by Norsk Tipping (Norwegian Lottery) that automatically allocates a portion of lottery ticket sales to registered sports clubs and community organizations. It's passive funding - clubs don't actively fundraise, instead receiving a percentage of lottery revenues.

**What it means:** "Grasrotandelen" means "the grassroots share" - government policy returning lottery profits to grassroots sports.

**Target Users:**
- Registered sports clubs (including golf clubs)
- Community organizations
- Non-profit associations
- Organizations with local/regional operations

### How It Works

#### Basic Mechanism
1. **Norsk Tipping Revenue**: A percentage of all lottery ticket sales goes to grassroots funding pool
2. **Automatic Distribution**: Funds are automatically allocated to registered organizations
3. **No Application**: Clubs don't apply - just register and receive funds
4. **Quarterly Payouts**: Payments distributed 3 times per year

#### Requirements to Participate
1. **Registration**: Club must be registered in Frivillighetsregisteret (Norwegian Volunteering Register)
2. **Approval**: Must be approved as a "grasrotmottaker" (grassroots recipient)
3. **Valid Account**: Bank account information registered in Frivillighetsregisteret
4. **Active Status**: Must maintain current registration status
5. **Local/Regional Operations**: Must serve local or regional area (not national)

#### How to Become a Grasrotmottaker
1. Register organization in Frivillighetsregisteret (Brønnøysundregisteret)
2. Register bank account number in Frivillighetsregisteret
3. Keep information current in Frivillighetsregisteret
4. Norsk Tipping automatically retrieves and processes info

### Fund Distribution Details

#### Payout Schedule
- **Three annual payouts**: Early January, May, and September
- **Minimum Threshold**: Must have accrued minimum kr. 300 to receive payment
- **Carryover**: Lower amounts carry over to next period
- **Automatic Process**: No action needed by club

#### Amount Calculation
- **Based on Revenue**: Percentage of lottery sales allocated to category
- **Number of Beneficiaries**: Divided among all registered recipients
- **Transparent**: Can view statistics on your organization

#### Usage Requirements
- **Flexible**: Clubs can use funds for most organizational purposes
- **Restrictions**: Cannot use for commercial activities or lobbying
- **Accountability**: Must maintain proper records

### Golf Club Benefits
- **Automatic Revenue**: Passive funding stream
- **Unrestricted Use**: Funds can support club operations
- **No Fundraising Required**: No need for active fundraising campaigns
- **Quarterly Income**: Predictable payment schedule

## Implementation Recommendations

### For Small Golf Clubs
1. **Core**: GolfBox (membership + bookings)
2. **Payment**: Vipps (primary) + Nets Easy
3. **Vending**: Nayax if applicable
4. **Funding**: Register for Grasrotandelen (passive income)
5. **Campaigns**: Use Spleis for specific projects

### For Mid-Size Golf Clubs
1. **Core**: GolfBox + ProPlanner (if offering lessons)
2. **Payment**: Nets Easy (primary) + Vipps (backup)
3. **POS**: Nayax terminals for all revenue points
4. **Funding**: Grasrotandelen + Spleis campaigns
5. **Yield**: Implement Yield Management module

### For Large Golf Clubs/Federations
1. **Core**: GolfBox (all modules)
2. **Payment**: Nets Easy + Vipps + custom integrations
3. **POS**: Comprehensive Nayax setup (multiple locations)
4. **Funding**: Diversified (Grasrotandelen + Spleis + sponsorships)
5. **Advanced**: Tournament modules, WebService API integration
