**Last Updated:** December 8, 2025

## 1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW

### What is Google for Nonprofits?

Google for Nonprofits is a comprehensive program that provides nonprofit organizations with free or significantly discounted access to Google's most powerful tools and technologies. The program is designed to help nonprofits spread their mission, engage supporters, fundraise online, and become more collaborative and productive.

**Available Through Google for Nonprofits:**
- Google Workspace (email, collaboration, productivity)
- Google Ad Grants (free advertising)
- YouTube Nonprofit Program (video engagement tools)
- Google Maps Platform credits (geographic tools)
- Google Earth Outreach tools

**Relevance for Norwegian Golf Clubs:**
Golf clubs registered as nonprofits/associations (foreninger, stiftelser) can access free Google Workspace licenses, enabling professional email, document collaboration, and team communication at no cost.

## 2. QUALIFICATION CRITERIA

### General Requirements

To be eligible for Google for Nonprofits, organizations must meet ALL of the following:

1. **Be registered as a charitable organization** in a country/region where Google for Nonprofits is available
2. **Meet specific country/region requirements** (see Norwegian requirements below)
3. **Pass verification by Goodstack** - Google's validation partner
4. **Accept Google for Nonprofits Terms of Service**

**Source:** [Retningslinjer for kvalifikation](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/3215869?hl=no)

### Organizations That Are NOT Eligible

The following organizations do NOT qualify for Google for Nonprofits:
- Government agencies or public entities
- Hospitals or healthcare organizations (though charitable divisions may qualify)
- Schools, academic institutions, or universities (though charitable divisions may qualify - see Google for Education instead)

**Source:** [Retningslinjer for kvalifikation](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/3215869?hl=no)

### Norwegian-Specific Requirements

For organizations registered in Norway, Google for Nonprofits accepts the following legal structures:

✅ **Qualifying Organization Types:**
- **Nonprofitvirksomhed (selskaper)** - Nonprofit companies/corporations
- **Fond (Stiftelser)** - Foundations/endowments
- **Forening (Sammenslutninger)** - Associations (most common for golf clubs)
- **Trossamfund (livssynssamfunn)** - Religious/belief communities
- **Organisations qualified to receive tax-deductible donations** - Organizations with tax-exempt status

**Important Note:** Golf clubs structured as "foreninger" (associations) are eligible if they are registered as nonprofit organizations and can provide proof of their nonprofit status.

**Documentation Needed:**
- Organization registration number (organisasjonsnummer)
- Tax ID (skatte-id)
- Proof of nonprofit registration

**Source:** [Retningslinjer for kvalifikation](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/3215869?hl=no)

## 3. GOOGLE WORKSPACE OFFERINGS

### What is Google Workspace for Nonprofits?

Google Workspace is Google's cloud-based suite of business applications that includes email, document collaboration, video conferencing, and more. The nonprofit program provides licenses at no cost or significant discounts.

### Tier Structure & Pricing

**Free Tier (Available through Google for Nonprofits):**
- **Google Workspace Standard Plan** - Fully FREE for qualifying nonprofits
  - Gmail with custom domain (yourorganization.com)
  - Google Drive (100 GB per user, shared storage up to 1 TB)
  - Google Docs, Sheets, Slides (unlimited storage for collaborative documents)
  - Google Meet (up to 24-hour group meetings for unlimited participants)
  - Google Calendar
  - Google Chat & Spaces
  - Advanced security and administration features
  - Mobile apps for iOS and Android
  - 99.9% uptime SLA

**Licensing:**
- Up to **1,000 free licenses** per organization
- Can add additional licenses at discounted nonprofit rates if needed beyond 1,000 users

**Comparison to Paid Plans:**
- **Google Workspace Business Starter (paid):** ~$6 USD/user/month
- **Google Workspace Business Standard (paid):** ~$12 USD/user/month
- **Google Workspace Business Plus (paid):** ~$18 USD/user/month
- **Nonprofit discount:** 100% FREE instead of these paid rates

**Return on Investment for Golf Clubs:**
For a typical Norwegian golf club with 50-200 members, this represents savings of:
- 50 users: ~€300-€3,600 USD/year
- 100 users: ~€600-€7,200 USD/year
- 200 users: ~€1,200-€14,400 USD/year

**Source:** [Google for Nonprofits portal](https://www.google.com/nonprofits/offerings/workspace/)

## 4. THE 5-STEP APPLICATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Review Qualification Requirements
**Time Required:** 30 minutes to 1 hour

1. Verify your organization meets ALL general requirements
2. Check Norway-specific requirements for your legal structure
3. Gather necessary documents:
   - Organization registration number (organisasjonsnummer)
   - Tax ID (skatte-id)
   - Proof of nonprofit status
   - Organization name, address, website
   - Primary contact person details

**Resources:**
- [Retningslinjer for kvalifikation](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/3215869?hl=no)
- [How to find your nonprofit organization registration/tax ID](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/12172927?hl=en&ref_topic=16049175)

**What to do:**
- Review the qualification checklist
- Prepare your documentation
- Ensure your organization has a registered website (helps during verification)

---

### Step 2: Submit Application for Google for Nonprofits Account
**Time Required:** 10-20 minutes

**How to Apply:**
1. Visit: [Google for Nonprofits Portal](https://www.google.com/nonprofits/)
2. Click "Kom godt i gang" (Get Started) button
3. If your organization already has a Google account, you'll go through a guided access request
4. If new to Google, create a Google Account for your organization first
5. Fill out the application form with:
   - Organization name (official legal name)
   - Organization address
   - Website URL
   - Tax ID / Registration number
   - Primary contact person
   - Phone number
   - Description of your organization's mission
   - Country/Region: Norway

**Important Notes:**
- Use your organization's official legal name
- Ensure the email address you use is accessible long-term
- The account holder becomes the primary administrator

**Source:** [5 trin til at komme godt i gang](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/3367631?hl=no)

---

### Step 3: Wait for Verification by Goodstack
**Time Required:** 2-14 business days (typical)

#### About Goodstack - Google's Verification Partner

**Who is Goodstack?**
- Goodstack (https://goodstack.org/) is an independent nonprofit organization that partners with Google
- They serve as the official validation partner for Google for Nonprofits globally
- They verify that applying organizations are legitimate, registered nonprofits

**What Goodstack Does:**
1. **Initial Review:** Examines your submitted documentation
2. **Organization Verification:** Checks registration databases in your country
3. **Follow-up Requests:** May contact you for additional information or documents
4. **Approval Decision:** Provides final verification to Google

**Typical Timeline:**
- **Expected:** 2-14 business days
- **Can extend if:** Additional documentation needed, unclear nonprofit status, registration conflicts
- **No fees:** Goodstack verification is free as part of the Google for Nonprofits program

**What Goodstack May Request:**
- Certified copies of registration documents
- Proof of tax-exempt status
- Leadership/board information
- Organization bylaws (vedtekter)
- Financial statements or annual reports
- Clarification on organizational structure

**Contact Information:**
- Visit: https://goodstack.org/ for more details about their verification process
- Support article: [Bliv verificeret af Goodstack](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/12016036)

**Pro Tip for Norwegian Golf Clubs:**
- Ensure your "organisasjonsnummer" (Norwegian organization number) is readily available
- Have your latest annual report or financial statement prepared
- If you've recently changed structure or name, document those changes

**How to Track Status:**
- Check status anytime at: [Google for Nonprofits Account Dashboard](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/1614637)
- Goodstack will contact you directly if they need additional information
- Google will email you when verification is complete

**Source:** 
- [5 trin til at komme godt i gang - Step 3](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/3367631?hl=no)
- [Bliv verificeret af Goodstack](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/12016036)

### Step 4: Activate Products in Google for Nonprofits
**Time Required:** 5-10 minutes per product

Once your organization is verified and approved, you can activate the specific Google products you need. Each product must be activated separately.

**How to Activate:**
1. Log into your Google for Nonprofits account
2. Navigate to the Products section
3. Select "Activate" for each product you want to use

**Available Products to Activate:**

| Product | What It Provides | Best For |
|---------|-----------------|----------|
| **Google Workspace** | Email, Docs, Drive, Meet, Chat, Calendar | Core communication & collaboration |
| **Google Ad Grants** | Free Google Search & Display advertising | Online visibility & fundraising campaigns |
| **YouTube Nonprofit Program** | Video monetization tools, nonprofit features | Sharing mission-focused videos |
| **Google Maps Platform** | Free API credits for mapping applications | Location-based services, event mapping |

**For Golf Clubs - Priority Recommendation:**
1. **First activate:** Google Workspace (core communication tool)
2. **Second activate:** Google Ad Grants (promote events, membership, courses)
3. **Optional:** YouTube Nonprofit (if creating video content)

## 5. TIMELINE EXPECTATIONS

### Full Timeline from Application to Activation

| Phase | Duration | What Happens |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| **Step 1: Preparation** | 1-2 days | Gather documents and prepare application |
| **Step 2: Application Submission** | Immediate | Submit to Google for Nonprofits portal |
| **Step 3: Goodstack Verification** | **2-14 business days** | Organization verification, may request additional documents |
| **Step 4: Activation** | Immediate upon approval | Activate individual products in your account |
| **Step 5: Implementation** | Ongoing | Set up email, configure Workspace, train users |
| **TOTAL TIME TO FULL SETUP** | **1-3 weeks** | From submission to email working on your domain |

### Key Timeline Facts:
- **Fastest possible:** ~3-5 business days (if all documents in order)
- **Typical case:** ~7-10 business days
- **Slowest cases:** Up to 14 business days (when additional verification needed)
- **After approval:** Immediate product activation
- **Email setup:** Usually working within 24-48 hours of activation

### What Causes Delays:
- Missing documentation
- Unclear nonprofit status in your country
- Recent organization changes (name, structure, address)
- Conflicting organization records
- Incomplete application form

### How to Avoid Delays:
✅ Complete all application fields accurately  
✅ Provide accurate organization registration number  
✅ Include all requested documentation upfront  
✅ Use official legal organization name  
✅ Ensure contact email is monitored daily  
✅ Respond quickly to Goodstack requests

## 7. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION & TERMS

### Service Terms
- Organizations must accept [Google for Nonprofits additional Terms of Service](https://support.google.com/nonprofits/answer/9004493?hl=da) as condition of eligibility
- Google reserves the right to suspend accounts that violate nonprofit program policies
- Regular compliance verification may occur

### Product Availability by Country
- Not all products are available in all countries
- For Norwegian organizations, you can check available products by logging into your Google for Nonprofits account
- Core Google Workspace is available in Norway

### Account Management
- One person serves as the primary account administrator
- Administrators can add additional users and manage licenses
- Multiple administrators can be designated for redundancy
- Account cannot be transferred; must reapply if organization ownership changes

### Security & Privacy
- All data is protected under Google Cloud security standards
- GDPR-compliant for EU/EEA organizations (including Norway)
- Two-factor authentication available and recommended
- Regular security audits by Google
